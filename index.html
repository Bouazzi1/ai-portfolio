How long does setup take? ▼
Standard chatbots: 3-5 days. AI-powered chatbots: 5-7 days. I can do rush delivery (1-2 days) for +50% if you need it faster.
Can I update the chatbot later? ▼
Yes! You'll get a simple config file where you can edit questions, answers, and settings anytime — no coding skills required. Or I can maintain it for you as part of a monthly plan.
What platforms does this work on? ▼
Any website. WordPress, Shopify, Wix, Squarespace, Webflow, or custom HTML. If it has a website, we can add a chatbot to it. WhatsApp and Telegram bots are also available.
Do you provide training & support? ▼
Yes. Every order includes 7 days of post-delivery support, setup documentation, and installation help. I also offer monthly maintenance packages for ongoing updates and optimization.
